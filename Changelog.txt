0.1

There are now only two teams

Completely new network backend
The underlying network code has been changed, (hopefully) leading to higher stability and fewer bugs

Balance changes

Gold gain per round increased to 160
Obstacles are now round-ish

Added a new spell: Arcane Bolt
Projectile
40 speed
0.4s cast time
10s cooldown
5 damage
5 knock factor
Clean hit on an enemy player refreshes the cooldown
Upgrade: Damage
Each hit increases the damage of the next arcane bolt in this chain by 10/15/20%.
Upgrade: Damage -> Knockback
Hitting all three arcane bolts in a row increases the knockback of your next spell by 50%.
Upgrade: Self Heal
Hitting an arcane bolt heals you for 3/6/9 health.
Upgrade: Self Heal -> Cooldown
Hitting an arcane bolt lowers all your cooldowns by 10%.

Added a new spell: Corrupting Bolt
Projectile
35 speed
0.5s cast time
10s cooldown
Deals 18 damage over 6 seconds
0 knock factor
Upgrade: Damage Reduction
Affected targets deal 10/20/30% less damage and healing
Upgrade: Damage Reduction -> Cooldown Debuff
Affected targets have 20% longer cooldowns
Upgrade: Amplify
Affected targets take 25% more damage from the next 1/2/3 direct damage spells
Upgrade: AoE Blast
Corrupting bolt now explodes in an AoE upon hit, affecting all targets within 4 units of impact point

Fireball
Cooldown nerfed to 8 seconds
Damage upgrade buffed to 1/2/3 (knock 0.8/1.6/2.4) from 0.6/1.2/1.8 (knock 0.45/0.9/1.35)
Cooldown upgrade buffed from 0.5 seconds to 1 second
Final blast upgrade cost nerfed to 120 from 80.

Heal
Heal duration upgrade buffed to 0.7/1.4/2.1 from 0.35/0.7/1.05 (total heal buffed to 26/31/36 from  23.45/26/28.35)
Fixed a bug with Heal damage reduction upgrade that caused it to amplify damage taken by 50% instead of reducing it

Life grip
Life grip no longer grants invulnerability
Life grip shield upgrade nerfed from 30 absorb to 20 and duration from 6 seconds to 3 seconds.
Life grip no longer lowers cooldowns
Life grip pulling speed nerfed from 65 to 50

Frost Prison
Fixed a bug causing Glacial Storm upgrade to immobilize instead of slow

Magma Blast
Magma Blast Damage upgrade buffed to 1.5/3/4.5 from 1/2/3
Magma Blast Lava Amplify upgrade replaced by Strength (increases knock factor by 3)
Magma Blast Cooldown upgrade removed
Magma blast Black Hole upgrade removed

Miscellaneous bug fixes
Name/Healthbar now hidden when dead
Players can now see invisible teammates

You no longer have to leave and re-enter powerup to start capping it again
Invisibility no longer grants invulnerability
Teams should now be properly displayed when in lobby
